Our Tech-Driven Solution

How JustiFlow can help courts work faster and smarter.

Core Features of JustiFlow

Digital Case Management

Every case has a unique digital record with parties, status, hearing dates, and documents in one place.

E-Filing & E-Records

Lawyers and parties can file documents online, reducing physical paperwork and file loss.

Smart Scheduling

Automated scheduling of hearings, prioritising old and high-priority cases using simple rules.

Automated Reminders

SMS / email alerts for upcoming hearings, document requirements, and status updates.

Dashboards for Judges

Visual dashboard showing pending, old, and urgent cases to help judges focus where it matters most.

Analytics & Reports

Simple reports on disposal rate, delays, and bottlenecks to support better decision-making.

Vision

JustiFlow is not about replacing humans, but about empowering courts with tools that reduce delays and bring justice closer to people.
