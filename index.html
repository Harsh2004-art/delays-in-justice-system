Delays in Justice System

Using Technology to Reduce Case Backlogs

Problem

Delays in the justice system are caused by case backlogs, slow processes, paperwork, and limited access to case information. This leads to prolonged trials and increased burden on courts.

Solution Using Technology

  • Digital Case Management Systems
  • E-filing of documents to reduce paperwork
  • AI-based legal research tools
  • Virtual hearings via video conferencing
  • Online Dispute Resolution platforms
  • Automation of routine administrative tasks

Goal

To build a technology-driven system that makes case handling faster, smarter, and more accessible.
