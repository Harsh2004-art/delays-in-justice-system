Delays in Justice System

Using technology to reduce case backlogs and prolonged trials.

Problem

Case backlogs, manual paperwork, and slow processes lead to prolonged trials and delayed justice. Courts struggle with tracking cases, scheduling hearings, and managing information efficiently.

Our Tech Solution

  • Digital case management system to track cases and hearings
  • E-filing and digital records instead of heavy paperwork
  • AI-based tools for faster legal research
  • Online dispute resolution and virtual hearings
  • Automation of routine tasks like reminders and status updates
